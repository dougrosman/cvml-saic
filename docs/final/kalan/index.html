Kalan Strauss

kalanstrauss.com | @kalanstrauss | @gangangannn

Kalan's work bridges the gap between nature and technology. These images and animations of clouds were the result of training a StyleGAN2 model on a data set of 3D renderings of clouds created in Unity. The neural network learns to resynthesize an already synthetic image of nature, paralleling our current quarantine-enforced indoor experience of nature (and everything else) as predominantly mediated through digtal technology.
